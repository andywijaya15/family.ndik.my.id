# Product Overview

Family Plan is a family financial management application that tracks income and expenses. The app allows users to:

- Manage transaction categories (income/expense types)
- Record and track financial transactions
- Track who paid for transactions and reimbursement status
- View financial data with pagination and filtering
- Authenticate via Supabase

The application is designed as a Progressive Web App (PWA) for mobile and desktop use, with a focus on family budget management and expense tracking.
